# 🛒 Retail Sales Intelligence Dashboard

An end-to-end Retail Sales Analytics Capstone Project focused on uncovering actionable business insights from sales, customers, products, returns, and store performance data.

This project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow — from raw data cleaning and SQL analysis to dashboard creation and business recommendations using Power BI.

---

# 📌 Project Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze retail business operations and identify:

- Revenue growth opportunities
- Customer purchasing behavior
- Product performance trends
- Return rate issues
- Store profitability challenges
- Strategic business recommendations

---

# 🚀 Business Problem

Retail businesses generate massive amounts of transactional data, but without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to:

- Track sales growth effectively
- Understand customer segments
- Identify underperforming products/stores
- Reduce return rates
- Improve operational efficiency

This project solves these challenges through data-driven insights and interactive dashboards.

# 📊 Key Insights

## 📈 Sales Performance

- Sales increased from approximately **302K (2024)** to **515K (2025)** showing nearly **70% growth**
- In-store sales contributed more than double the online sales revenue
- Peak sales occurred during **Jan–Mar 2025**

### Insight:
The business is growing rapidly but depends heavily on physical stores, creating a channel imbalance risk.

---

## 👥 Customer Insights

- Total Customers: **800**
- Highest revenue generated by customers aged **26–45**
- Lowest contribution from the **16–25** age segment
- West region generated the highest revenue

### Insight:
Mid-age working professionals form the core customer base, while younger customers represent a major untapped opportunity.

---

## 📦 Product Performance

- Highest profit category: **Personal Care**
- Lowest profit category: **Home & Kitchen**
- Return rates remained consistently around **16–17%**

### Insight:
Consistent return rates across categories indicate possible operational or expectation-related issues rather than isolated product failures.

---

# 🔁 Returns Analysis

- Total Returns: **200**
- East region recorded the highest returns
- Return rates fluctuated between **3%–14%**

### Possible Causes:
- Product mismatch
- Delivery/service quality
- Customer expectation gaps

---

# 🏬 Store Performance

- Total Stores: **61**
- Online stores generated the highest profits
- Several stores showed high operational costs with low profitability

### Insight:
Store-level optimization is required to improve efficiency and profitability.

---

# ⚠️ Key Business Risks

- Over-reliance on in-store sales
- High and unstable return rates
- Uneven store profitability
- Underutilized younger customer segment

---

# 💡 Strategic Recommendations

## Revenue Growth
- Expand online marketing campaigns
- Target younger customers using personalized offers

## Returns Reduction
- Improve product descriptions
- Enhance quality control
- Introduce return reason tracking dashboards

## Store Optimization
- Identify high-cost, low-profit stores
- Replicate strategies from top-performing stores

## Product Strategy
- Focus more on high-performing categories
- Improve weaker-performing product categories
